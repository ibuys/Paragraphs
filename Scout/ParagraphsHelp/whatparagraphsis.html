What Paragraphs Is

Paragraphs is the embodiment of a dream. I've put my heart and soul into the application. I know that not everything in it is perfect, but I'm working hard on making it as good as I possibly can. My company, Farmdog Co. is built on the concept that there is a better way to live. That there are ways that a person can make a living and still have some control over his or her destiny. Farmdog is made of dreams.

Paragraphs is a tool to create Web sites. A desktop blogging application. I hope you like it, and I hope that Paragraphs helps you to share who you are and what your thoughts are with the world. Everyone has something to share, a story to tell. Paragraphs is here to help you share yours. There is much, much more coming.
